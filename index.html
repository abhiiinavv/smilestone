Frequently Asked Questions

Explore answers to frequently asked questions about our treatments and services

Does a root canal hurt?

A root canal is performed under local anesthesia, ensuring a painless procedure. Any post-treatment discomfort is typically mild and can be managed with prescribed or over-the-counter pain relievers.

What’s the best way to replace a missing tooth—bridge, implant, or denture?

The best option depends on bone health, budget, and personal preference:
Dental Implants: Long-lasting, function like natural teeth.
Bridges: Fixed replacement supported by adjacent teeth.
Dentures: Removable option for multiple missing teeth. A consultation will determine the most suitable choice for you.

Are dental X-rays safe?

Yes, modern dental X-rays use minimal radiation exposure, making them safe. Digital X-rays further reduce exposure while providing essential diagnostic information.

Do braces or aligners cause pain?

Mild discomfort is common, especially after adjustments or when switching aligner trays. This usually subsides within a few days and can be managed with over-the-counter pain relief if needed.

What is biomimetic filling in dentistry?

A biomimetic filling is a tooth-colored, adhesive restoration that mimics the natural structure and function of a tooth. Unlike traditional fillings, it bonds strongly to the tooth, reducing the risk of fractures and improving long-term durability.

What is a rubber dam? Benefits of a rubber dam?

A rubber dam is a thin sheet of latex or non-latex material used to isolate the tooth during dental procedures.
Benefits:
* The treatment area dry and free from saliva.
* Prevents contamination and improves bonding success.
* Protects the patient from swallowing or inhaling small instruments or debris.
* Increases the efficiency of the procedure.

How can I manage dental anxiety??

Dental anxiety can be managed through open communication, relaxation techniques, and gradual exposure to treatment. Bringing headphones, practicing deep breathing, or scheduling a consultation beforehand can also help ease concerns.

What are the sterilization protocols at Smile Stone Dental Clinic?

At Smile Stone Dental Clinic, we follow strict sterilization protocols,
Including:
* Autoclaving all instruments at high temperatures.
* Using disposable materials where necessary.
* Disinfecting dental chairs and surfaces after every patient.
* Following the four-tier sterilization process (Cleaning, Packing, Sterilization, and Storage).
